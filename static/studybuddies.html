Find study buddies, study together

StudyBuddies is a web application to help students find others to study with and to develop study habits. Sometimes students don't know others in some of the classes that they're taking. StudyBuddies aims to remedy this by allowing people to connect, meet and study. Students can add classes they're taking to automatically search for study groups. if no study groups are available, students can create one.
